# Top 10 Anime Genres: Highly Faithful Adaptations

1. **Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood**
2. **Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba**
3. **Hunter x Hunter (2011)**
4. **Jujutsu Kaisen**
5. **Bleach**

1. **Frieren: Beyond Journey's End**

3. **Dorohedoro**
